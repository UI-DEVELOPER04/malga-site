Our Mission

At Malga, we exist to provide the perfect platform for people across the world to form meaningful and life-enhancing connections.

Our Culture

Everyday people: We 'get' people and know what strong and lasting relationships rely on: clear communication, passion, empathy, mutual respect and fun. Virtual or face-to-face, we always find a way to allow these values to shine.

Nothing ventured, nothing gained: As a business, we know that nothing stands still; across our customers needs, evolving technologies and methods of communicating virtually. Our Teams are encouraged to be constantly curious, thinking about new and improved methods of allowing relationships to thrive.

Inspiring Experiences

We are a software company but a user's experience and feelings always drive our solutions. A user experience built on the confidence to interact, connect and communicate always comes before processes, outcomes and technology.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer assistance in managing the virtual events?

The simple answer is YES. We offer a self-service model or a fully supported and managed event (and anything in-between!)

What is the average turn around for a virtual event?

At present, the average turnaround for hosting a virtual event on the Malga platform is 4 weeks.

Does Malga offer reporting, analytics and integrations?

Yes, Malga has a suite of reports and analytics for you to review. It has many existing features but also has the ability to integrate with other legacy tech.

Do I have to have a separate streaming company to help with the live stream?

No - we are able to help with this. However, you do have the option to use external streaming if you wish.
