Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer assistance in managing the virtual events?

The simple answer is YES. We offer a self-service model or a fully supported and managed event (and anything in-between!)

What is the average turn around for a virtual event?

At present, the average turnaround for hosting a virtual event on the Malga platform is 4 weeks.

Does Malga offer reporting, analytics and integrations?

Yes, Malga has a suite of reports and analytics for you to review. It has many existing features but also has the ability to integrate with other legacy tech.

Do I have to have a separate streaming company to help with the live stream?

No - we are able to help with this. However, you do have the option to use external streaming if you wish.
